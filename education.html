Educational Focus: The Ethics of Online Kindness

Just as we are called to love our neighbors offline, the principle of online kindness is essential to ethical digital practices. Our words carry weight, especially on public platforms.

Our Commitment to Digital Integrity

  • Honesty in Digital Creation: Always give credit where it's due (no plagiarism) and represent facts truthfully.
  • Kindness in Comments: Respond with empathy and respect, avoiding conflict and negativity.
  • Privacy Protection: Respect the personal boundaries and data of others online.
"Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ God forgave you." - (Ephesians 4:32, applying it digitally)
